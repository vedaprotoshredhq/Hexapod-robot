## Assembly Notes

Each leg requires:
- 1× Corex (coxa)
- 1× Femur (femur)
- 1× Tibita (tibia)
- 3× MG90S servo motors
- Fasteners (2mm screws)

Total for complete hexapod:
- 6× Corex segments
- 6× Femur segments
- 6× Tibita segments
- 18× MG90S servos
- 2× Body panels (top & bottom)
- Multiple horn supports

## Print Order Recommendation

1. Print all body components first (chassis, panels)
2. Print all leg segments (6 sets of Corex, Femur, Tibita)
3. Print supporting brackets and accessories
4. Assembly after all components are printed and cleaned
